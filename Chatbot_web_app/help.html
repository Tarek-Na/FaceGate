Frequently Asked Questions

Our AI chatbot is powered by Google's Gemini model with Qwen:0.5B as a fallback, providing intelligent responses about University of Balamand services, campus information, directions, and visitor procedures. The AI can understand natural language questions and provide accurate, helpful responses in real-time.

Click the "Submit Visitor Request" button or tell the AI chatbot you want to visit. You'll need to provide your name, phone number, visit purpose, person/department you're visiting, and expected duration. ID number is optional. The system will create a ticket and notify security for approval.

The AI assistant can provide comprehensive information about University of Balamand Koura Campus including: all 7 faculties (Arts & Sciences, Engineering, Business, Health Sciences, Medicine, Law, Theology), building locations and descriptions, contact information, directions from major cities, library hours, cafeteria services, parking information, and emergency contacts.

Our face recognition system achieves 99.7% accuracy under optimal conditions. It uses advanced AI algorithms that analyze over 80 unique facial features, making it highly reliable for campus security. The system works effectively in various lighting conditions and can recognize faces from different angles, with real-time processing in under 2 seconds.

After submission, your request is assigned a unique ticket ID and sent to the security team for review. Security personnel will verify your information and either approve or deny the request within 15 minutes. You'll receive a notification with the decision, and if approved, you can proceed to the main gate with your ID for entry.

From Beirut: Take the highway north, exit at Amioun, and follow the university signs (about 1.5 hours). From Tripoli: Take the coastal highway south and exit at the Balamand interchange (about 45 minutes). From Amioun: The campus is just 5 minutes from the city center, follow the university signs. GPS coordinates: 34.4236° N, 35.8303° E.

The campus is open 24/7 for students and staff with valid access. Main gates operate 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM for visitors. Library: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM (weekdays), 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (weekends). Cafeteria: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM. Medical Center: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM (weekdays). Security services available 24/7.

Yes, your data is highly secure. We use military-grade encryption for all personal information, comply with GDPR and Lebanese privacy laws, and store data on secure university servers. Facial recognition data is encrypted and never shared with third parties. Visitor requests are automatically deleted after 30 days, and you can request data deletion at any time.

For emergencies, use the red "Emergency Contact" button on the main screen to immediately alert campus security. You can also call security directly at +961 6 930 255. For medical emergencies, contact the campus medical center at +961 6 930 280. Security personnel are stationed throughout campus 24/7 and can respond within 3-5 minutes.
